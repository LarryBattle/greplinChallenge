Objective

Find the amount of times a number in a list of numbers is formed from the summation of 2 or more numbers.
For example, for an input of: `(1, 2, 3, 4, 6)`, the answer is 4, since the list of formed numbers are as followed.

  1. 1 + 2 = 3
  2. 1 + 3 = 4
  3. 2 + 4 = 6
  4. 1 + 2 + 3 = 6
